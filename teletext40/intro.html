teletext40 was a project to make a new teletext service online that anyone could contribute to. It was started in Setember 2014. It was only available on the web, was a little clunky, required people to email their frames to us, but it generated the frames you see below. Some of them were done by editors of t40, some donated from other projects, and some just done at events we'd hold to get people making frames.

The teletext community grew since then. Now we have the excellent Teefax, which can be uploaded to by anyone and can be displayed on real teletext televisions using a Raspberry Pi.
